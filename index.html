Deliver papers!
Reset Button
Play Button
Move the delivery person with WASD keys, throw papers with Q and E to throw left or right. Delivering paper to subscribers (green-bordered houses) will earn you money, while delivering to non-subscribers (red-bordered hosues) will lose money! You can only score or lose points once per house - signified by the house border turning yellow. Extra newspaper can be picked up on the map (white box) The game will end when you run out of newspaper.
